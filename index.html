CARD GAME


It’s a memory + sequence game:
There are 10 cards placed randomly face down.
Each card has a number 1 to 10.
The goal is to pick cards in order (1 → 10).

How it works:
 You click a card → it flips and reveals a number.
If it’s the correct next number, it stays open
If it’s wrong, all opened cards flip back face down

Objective:
 Remember positions of numbers
Successfully reveal all cards in order without mistakes
